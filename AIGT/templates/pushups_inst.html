Instruction

Start by assuming a face-down position on the floor with your hands placed slightly wider than shoulder-width apart. Position your toes on the ground, creating a straight line from your head to your feet while engaging your core muscles. Lower your body by bending your elbows, keeping them close to your sides, until your chest is just above the ground. Push through your palms and extend your arms to lift your body back up, returning to the starting position. Repeat the lowering and pushing up motion, focusing on maintaining a straight back and proper form throughout the exercise. Gradually increase the number of repetitions as your strength improves.
