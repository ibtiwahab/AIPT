ABOUT US

Personal AI Trainer is a web-based application where user can join free of cost and work on different exercises provided there. User will also be able to read some blogs that will also help him making his work out better and advise him how to make his body fit. User can also see the history of reps he did in the past so he can catch up how well he is doing now. This software would help people who don’t have enough time to go to gym and do exercise.

  • • Users can train in their own comfort zone instead of going to gym.
  • • Free of cost so people don’t have to spend money on going to gym.
  • • Guiding the user on how to do exercises correctly while avoiding any muscle injury.

Outcome of this project is to make sure user remain in good fitness without causing any muscle injury and make sure people can easily do exercises in their own comfort instead of paying gym fees. Also, to make sure the users are performing correct exercises in the right posture instead of hiring a trainer to make sure they are doing correct exercise
