Instructions

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, grasping the barbell with an underhand grip (palms facing upward). Let your arms hang straight down by your sides, keeping your elbows close to your body. Engage your core and keep your back straight throughout the exercise. Keeping your upper arms stationary, exhale and slowly curl the barbell upward towards your shoulders by contracting your biceps. Continue the upward movement until your forearms are vertical and the barbell is at shoulder level.
